About

Safety Squad, India's first all rounder safety system, transforms the way train rides and handles mishaps. It does for accidents what noise cancellation does for for noise, mitigating rail track's unpredictability within a few seconds. Relying on a combination of IoT and Web Technologies, SafetySquad detects objects in rail tracks and alerts the driver, thus giving time to react.Our drowsiness detection model ensures that drivers are awake, thus providing unmatched security and stability.
